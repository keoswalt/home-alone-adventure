Home Alone: The Remix

Can you help Kevin's mom make better choices this time?

What would you do if you were Kate McCallister?

In the classic Christmas film Home Alone, eight-year-old Kevin McCallister is inexplicably forgotten by his parents and siblings when they fly to Paris for the holidays. In this choose-your-own-adventure story, you get to help Kevin’s mom, Kate, make (hopefully) less questionable decisions. Or just do exactly what she does in the movie. It’s up to you!
